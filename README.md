# Daily Song Journal 🎵

A simple app where you save one song that meant something to you today.

## What it does

- Save a song - write the song name and the artist
- Say why it matters - write a short note about why this song fits your day
- Pick a mood - choose how you were feeling (Happy, Sad, Calm, etc.)
- Add a memory - optionally write who the song reminds you of
- Mark favorites - star the songs you love most
- See a calendar - look back at every song you saved, day by day
- Track your streak - see how many days in a row you've journaled
